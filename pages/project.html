Case Study

Ghana Trotro Transit

Revolutionising public transit navigation, one route at a time.

Client
Ghana Trotro Transit
Services
Mobile App, Backend, Design, Web, Admin
Year
2025
The Challenge

Ghana's trotro network has no official map.

Trotros move millions of commuters every day, but routes, stations and fares live entirely in the shared knowledge of drivers, mates and regular riders. For newcomers, and even for daily commuters heading somewhere unfamiliar, there was no reliable way to plan a trip in advance.

The Solution

Real-time mapping, built with the people who ride.

We built a mobile app that plots trotro routes across the country, surfaces live fare and station data, and puts trip planning in a simple, uncluttered interface — designed to be usable on entry-level Android devices and patchy mobile data.

Core feature

Route search that thinks like a local.

Enter any two points in Accra — or beyond — and the app surfaces the fastest and most affordable trotro combinations, including transfers, in seconds.

More on board
